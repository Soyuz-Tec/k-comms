# Error Budget Policy

- Error budget equals allowed unreliability under the approved SLO.
- Burn-rate alerts use multiple windows to detect both rapid and slow consumption.
- When budget is exhausted, reliability work and risk reduction take priority over discretionary launches.
- Exemptions require an identified owner, duration, customer impact statement, and executive approval.
- Incidents that do not consume a chosen SLI may still require action when security, data integrity, or contractual commitments are affected.
