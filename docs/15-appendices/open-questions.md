# Open Questions

## Product and protocol

- Is end-to-end encryption required at launch?
- Is voice/video part of the platform or an external integration?
- Which clients and minimum versions are supported?
- Is federation required, and under which protocol?
- What are maximum conversation and channel sizes?

## Reliability and scale

- Peak concurrent connections and messages per second?
- Required RPO and RTO by failure scope?
- Is acknowledged-message durability required across regional loss?
- What graceful-degradation behavior is acceptable?

## Security and compliance

- Required jurisdictions, residency, retention, legal hold, and audit standards?
- What support access to message content is permitted?
- Is customer-managed encryption required?

## Delivery

- Target launch date and phased rollout?
- Existing systems or clients to integrate/migrate?
- Self-hosted edition or SaaS only?
- Budget and staffing constraints?
